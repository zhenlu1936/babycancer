- REPL现场操作: ./scripts/repl_demo_setup.sh 后运行 ./target/release/babycancer，再参考 ./scripts/repl_demo_commands.sh

- 95-110s 对比亮点：压缩前后大小、CRC32完整性
- 110-120s 收尾：4个关键词 + 行动号召
